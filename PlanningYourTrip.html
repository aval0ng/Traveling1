Believe it or not, I believe safe, fun, and traveling that is friendly on the paycheck is a skill that can be acquired and developed. There are some tried and true methods to planning a vacation, or a business trip etc., and these can be learned with the right mentality, just like math or computer science.

Little Hacks

Find a place to visit!

This may be the hardest part of the whole process! I usually like to ask myself WHY I am traveling this time. Is it for tourism? Is it to relax? Is it to spend time with family? This will help determine what one should do (don't go hiking on a strenuous mountain if you're trying to relax), and ultimately, where one should go.

Plan at 3 months ahead (to get the best deals)

Planning ahead allows for time to search for flights and browse various hotel booking websites before settling down. This can happen over the course of weeks.

Research

Research is light and simple. Think about the weather and how that will affect what you pack.

Start packing a week before you leave

I'm suggesting you take your luggage out a week early for long trips, especially to rural places where it is inconvenient to access stores. Throw things into the luggage as you think of them.

General time to start packing

3 weeks + length 2 weeks + length 1 week or less
1.5 weeks before 1 week before 3 days before (depending on access to stores)

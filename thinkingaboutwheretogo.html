I live about 5 miles from Boston, so I've lived very close to the city my entire life. While cities are excellent and fun places to visit, I actually enjoy visiting places where I can be immersed in nature. I enjoy lakes, mountains, glaciers, forests, hiking, canoeing, sunsets, and a variety of outdoor activities. These places help me relax and destress. I like to think about why I am traveling. I try to find places that suit this reason
